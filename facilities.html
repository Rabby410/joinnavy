Facilities

Facilities

The Bangladesh Navy can call upon an arsenal of warships and weaponry to meet any challenge. From aircraft carriers and assault ships to agile patrol boats and survey vessels, our ships are ready and capable.

NAVAL ACADEMY

North Potenga, Chittagong
Tel: 2501207-14

Location : Bathed by the river Karnaphuli on the east and sheltered on the south by the Bay of Bengal, BNA stands in a panoramic natural picturesque in an isolated landscape of the country. It is surrounded by heart-snatching lush green vegetation in Patenga sea-beach, 14 km south of the city centre of the hustling port city of Chittagong.
Address : The Commandant and Commanding Officer Bangladesh Naval Academy Patenga, Chittagong, Bangladesh Tel : 880-31-2501200 (Direct) 880-31-2501207-16 (PABX) Fax : 880-31-2501206
Communication : Well connected by rail, road and air from Dhaka.
Training Facilities : One of the premiere training institutes of Bangladesh, BNA deals with the immense task of fulfilling national policy and committed for developing commissioned officers to lead the future Bangladesh Navy. Primarily, BNA training has four dimensions; Academics, General Naval Training. Its facilities include Seamanship Model Room, Chart Room, Auditorium, Sailing and Pulling facilities, NBCD simulator, Planetarium for Astro Navigation, Gymnasium and Health Club etc. Academic training facilities include physics lab, Language lab, Computer lab, Library etc. BNA also conducts basic Computer Course, Branch Rank Common Course, Language Course and Junior Staff Course for BN and overseas officers.
Accommodation Facilities : Well-furnished Gun Room for Cadets and Under Training Officers.
Recreation : Adequate sports and other recreational facilities including golf are available.
Medical Facilities : First aid centre with qualified doctors is available withing the campus. Combined Military Hospital, BNS PATENGA is only a few kilometers from the Academy.
Heritage of Chittagong : Chittagong is the second largest city of Bangladesh and a busy international seaport. It is an ideal vacation spot. Its green hills and forests, broad sandy beaches and dine cool climate always attract the holiday makers. Chittagong combines the busy and active seaport with the soothing quietness of a charming hill town.

BNS ISSA KHAN

New Mooring ,Chittagong
Tel: 740391-9

Location : BNS ISSA KHAN is located 8 km south of the city centre of Chittagong is near to the rever Karnaphuli. It is also very close to the Chittagong International Airport.
Address : The Commanding Officer BNS ISSA KHAN New Mooring, Chittagong, Bangladesh. Tel : 880-31-740391(741770-9) 880-31-714971 (up to 74)
Communication : Well connected by rail, road and air form Dhaka.
Training Facilities : The largest training establishment of Bangladesh Bavy, BNS ISSA KHAN mainly conducts the basic training of Seaman officers and sailors. Training is conducted under different schools like Gunnery, TAS, Communication, Navigation, Diving, NBCD and Hydrography. The course at schools enhances the level of theoretical knowledge of trainees and thereby equip them to undertake challenging task onboard ships. It is to be3 noted that all schools are equipped with modern training aids to meet the challenges to the modern warfare.
Accommodation Facilities : Well-furnished Wardroom for Under Trainee Officers and dormitory for sailors.
Recreation : Adequate sports and other recreational facilities including swimming pool are available.
Medical Facilities : At Combined Military Hospital- BNS PATENGA.

BNS TITUMIR

Town Khalishpur, Khulna
Tel: 761420-9

Location : BNS TITUMIR is at Khulna, 3 km from the city centre. Khulna is one of the 4 major Metropolitan cities in the country located in the southwest of Bangladesh with Industrial set up. Washed by rivers and canals, the city is well connected to the world through her port located in a nearby township of Mongla. The city also host the largest ship building industry in the country.
Address : The Commanding Officer BNS TITUMIR Khalishpur, Khunla, Bangladesh. Phone : 880-41-761421 (up to 92)
Communication : Well connected by road, rail and air (at Jessore).
Training Facilities : Home of logistics training, BNS TITUMIR mainly conducts professional training for the supply branch officers and sailors at different level of their career. School of Logistic and Management (SOLAM) is specialized to equip trainees for their further appointments onboard ships and ashore. new Entry Training School (NETS) is also co-located and conducts the Boot Camp training of newly recruits.
Accommodation Facilities : Well furnished Wardroom for under training officers and dormitory for sailors.
Recreation : Adequate Sports and others recreational facilities are available which include Swimming Pool, Tennis, Squash, Golf, Billiard etc. Besides School of Logistic and Management will provide limited internet facilities with minimum charges.
Medical Facilities : BN Hospital BNS UPASHAM and CMH at Jessore.
Heritage of Khulna : Khulna is one of the old trade centres of the country. The world’s largest mangrove forest Sundarban lies in this area. Khulna is a moderately populated metropolitan city, where living expenses are relaticely cheaper. Groceries including fresh fruits of different kind grow in the area in abundance. Fishes of both sweet and sea water species are available in this part of the count throughout the year. Khulna’s delicious cuisines are mouth watering and thus attract visitors from different part of the country. Royal Bengal Tigers and Spotted Deer of Sundarban attract tourists.

BNS SHAHED MOAZZOM

Kapti, Rangamati Hill District, Chittagong
Tel: 03529-56382/3

Location : Located in the hilly terrain of Kaptai under Rangamati Hill District, The southeastern part of Bangladesh. The base is situated on the bank of fascinating artificial lake of Kaptai, the largest fresh water reservoir of Bangladesh.
Address : The Commanding Officer BNS SHAHEED MOAZZAM kaptai, Rangamati Hill District, Bangladesh. Phone : 044 34491592
Communication : Overseas students shall arrive upto Chittagong by air. There after about two hours drive by road.
Training Facilities : the premier technical training establishment of Bangladesh Navy, BNS SHAHEED MOAZZAM, has three Schools named Engineering School, Electrical School and Seamanship School to conduct training of the officers and sailors. The spectrum of officers training encompasses Executive Officers Basic Course (XOBC), Marine Engineering Specialisation Course (MESC) conducted bvy Engineering School and Long Electrical Course (LEC) conducted by Electrical School. The sailors professional training is also conducted at these Schools.
Accommodation Facilities : Well-furnished Wardroom/Gunroom for Officers and dormitory for sailors.
Recreation : Adequate sports and other recreational facilities are available, attractive facilities for boating, skiing and swimming are also abailable.
Medical Facilities : First aid centre with qualified doctors are available within the base. Hospital facilities at combined military hospital BNS PATENGA and CMH Chittagong are also available when required.
Heritage of Rangamati : Rangamati is a hilly town, which is known for its panoramic view. With enchanting scenic beauty, Kaptai lake also provides sweet water fishes in abundance. Different types of fresh fruits also grow in the hilly terrain of the area. Largest Reserve Forest of the country is in this area. The town is lightly populated and most of the inhabitants are of tribal origin. The tribal cultures dominate the age old heritage of Rangamati.

BNS NIRVIK

School of SWADS Center
Tel: 01769-724730

New Mooring ,Chittagong

SMWT

South of Chittagong
Phone : 880-31-741851

Location : It is located 8km south of Chittagong city center and situated within the campus of BNS ISSA KHAN.
Address : The Commanding Officer SMWT New Mooring, Chittagong, Bangladesh. Phone : 880-31-741851
Communication : Well connected by rail, road and air from Dhaka.
Training Facilities : The School is equipped with the State of Art Technology “Action Speed Tactical Trainer” (ASTT). It facilitates simulator based training for naval officers in planning and execution of different war/short of war situation. It also provides various options exercising the basic procedures of tactics and reporting procedure for the young Officers and Sailors. Besides, the SMWT also has a “Bridge Simulator” which provides facilities for the ships Command Team to practice navigational procedure under different weather conditions. The School also has Sonar and EW Simulator for facilitating training of Officers and Sailors.
Accommodation Facilities : Well-furnished Wardroom for Trainee Officers and dormitory for sailors of BNS ISSA KHAN.
Recreation : Adequate sports and other recreational facilities are available including swimming pool in the area.
Medical Facilities : At Combined Military Hospital BNS PATENGA.
